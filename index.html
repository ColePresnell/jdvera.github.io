About Me

My name is James Vera. I’ve lived most of my life in the Austin area. I grew up in Kyle, and now live in northern Austin. The city is awesome, but Texas is always so hot during most of the year. If I ever moved, I would likely move north, out of Texas.

I went to college at Texas A&M University. My dad also went to A&M, and my brother went to UT. While I love A&M and enjoy the rivalry between the schools, I’ve never really had that “hatred” toward UT like some people have towards rival schools.

I previously worked for a SaaS company, doing tier-1 technical support. From that and some of the intro programming classes I took in college, I feel I developed a solid foundation regarding programming concepts and the importance of appearance/syntax. However, since I do not have any more formal education in programming, I could not move into the positions I was more interested in, which is why I’m attending this class.

In my free time, I mostly just watch YouTube. I do like video games, but I don’t really care for the popular RTS/FPS online games. I have good strategy, but not great reaction time. I also really enjoy playing Magic: the Gathering, which can be a very complicated game. My brother and many of our friends also play Magic, and are software engineers/developers. They’ve said that me being good at Magic should help me do well in this field too, so I guess we’ll see.
